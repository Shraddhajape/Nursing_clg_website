About us

Welcome to Our Campus

Vijaysingh Mohite Patil school and College of nursing and medical research institute, Akluj ,believes in a holistic approach to education, blending theoretical knowledge with practical experience. Our state-of-the-art facilities and simulation labs provide a simulated clinical setting where you can gain hands-on skills and prepare for the challenges that lie ahead. Additionally, our strong partnerships with renowned healthcare institutions offer valuable clinical placement opportunities, ensuring you receive comprehensive training and exposure to diverse healthcare settings.
